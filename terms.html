2. Membership

2.1 Eligibility

You must be at least 16 years old to become a Da Gym member. Members aged 16–17 require written parental or guardian consent. We reserve the right to verify your age at any time.

2.2 No Contract

Da Gym memberships are rolling monthly agreements with no minimum term. You are free to cancel at any time, subject to the notice period set out in Section 5. There are no long-term contracts or cancellation fees.

2.3 Membership Types

We offer three membership tiers — Off-Peak, Core, and Plus — each providing different levels of access. Full details of what is included in each tier are available at dagym.com and at our gym locations. Prices are displayed clearly at point of sale and may vary by gym location.

2.4 Payment

Membership fees are collected monthly by Direct Debit or recurring card payment on or around the same date each month. You are responsible for ensuring your payment details are kept up to date. If a payment fails, we will attempt to collect again and may suspend access to our gyms until payment is received.

3. Gym Access & Use

3.1 Access

Access to our gyms is provided by means of a PIN code or biometric scan. You must not share your access credentials with any other person. Unauthorised entry may result in immediate termination of your membership.

3.2 Gym Rules

  • Appropriate gym attire, including clean training shoes, must be worn at all times.
  • Wipe down all equipment after use with the cleaning products provided.
  • Re-rack weights and return equipment to its designated location.
  • Mobile phones may be used for music, but loud calls or filming of other members without consent is not permitted.
  • Bags must be stored in lockers — do not bring bags onto the gym floor.
  • Food is not permitted on the gym floor. Water in a sealed, non-glass container is allowed.
  • Aggressive, abusive, or threatening behaviour toward staff or other members will not be tolerated and will result in immediate membership termination.

3.3 Classes

Group exercise classes are included in Core and Plus memberships. Class bookings can be made via the Da Gym app or website. Off-Peak members may access classes during off-peak hours only. We reserve the right to cancel or reschedule classes and will notify members where possible.

3.4 Guest Access

Guest passes may be available for purchase at certain gyms. Guests must be accompanied by a member at all times and must comply with these Terms and Conditions and our Gym Rules.
