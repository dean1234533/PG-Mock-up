About This Gym

Da Gym London Stratford

One of East London's biggest and best-equipped gyms, located in the heart of Stratford Centre — steps from Stratford Station and Westfield.

We've got a massive free weights area with Olympic lifting platforms, 220+ pieces of cardio and resistance kit, cables, and a functional training zone — plus a full class timetable all included in your membership.

Open 24/7, no long-term contracts. Start today and cancel anytime. A Women's Workout Space is coming soon, with a dedicated area for lifting, cables, and more.
