Our Mission

Fitness shouldn't be a privilege

Da Gym was born from a frustration with expensive gym memberships, long contracts, and overcrowded facilities that most people couldn't access or afford.

We set out to build something different — world-class facilities at honest prices, no contracts, open around the clock. We believe that everyone deserves access to a great gym, regardless of their budget or schedule.

Today we're the UK's largest gym operator, but our mission hasn't changed: to make fitness accessible, inclusive, and empowering for every single person who walks through our doors.
